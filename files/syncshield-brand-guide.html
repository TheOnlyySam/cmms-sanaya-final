09 — Brand Guidelines
Usage Rules & Clear Space
✓ Do
  • Use the logo on approved navy, steel blue, white, or ice backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "S" letter around the logo
  • Use Orbitron for all headlines and UI labels
  • Keep the Electric Teal accent for interactive and highlight elements
  • Use Alert Orange exclusively for urgent maintenance notifications
  • Scale the icon proportionally and never below 22px height
  • Use the horizontal logo lockup for documents and headers
✗ Don't
  • Distort, stretch, or rotate the logo or shield icon
  • Use the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without an overlay
  • Change the shield color or sync arrow color to anything other than approved palette
  • Add shadows, gradients, or outlines to the logo
  • Use fonts other than Orbitron, Exo 2, or Share Tech Mono in brand materials
  • Reproduce "SyncShield" as two separate words or all lowercase
  • Use the Alert Orange as a general background or decorative color
